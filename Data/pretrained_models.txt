The unoptimized pretrained models are approximately 1GB of size. Please get them from https://github.com/mgharbi/hdrnet
and put them into the folder "Data/pretrained_models".

Thus, the program is accompanied only by some smaller datasets from https://github.com/mgharbi/hdrnet.
